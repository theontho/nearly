Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

The short version

Nearly does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Your files stay on your Mac.

Data Collection

Nearly collects no personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry of any kind. The app operates entirely offline and locally on your device.

Files

All documents you create or edit are stored locally on your Mac. Nearly only accesses files you explicitly open. No files are uploaded, synced, or shared with any server.

Third-Party Services

Nearly does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Network Access

The app includes a network entitlement solely for rendering web content in the preview pane (via WKWebView). No data is sent to external servers.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page. Given that there is nothing to collect, we don't expect it to.
